Where I'm From

I originally hail from Hesston, KS, a small town about 40 minutes north of Wichita. I graduated from Hesston High School in 2007. I moved to Manhattan, KS where I attended Kansas State University earning a BS in Management Information Systems. After working in Manhattan for a couple years I moved to Wichita, KS.

Where I've Worked

My first professional Job was for Information Technology Services at K-State doing Desktop Support. After getting a great opportunity, I moved to Wichita, KS where I worked for Conco Construction as a Systems Administrator. I currently work at Wichita State University as a Data Warehousing Analyst.

What's Fun

I enjoy riding my bicycle and go for a few rides a week. I love to keep up with tech news, from the newest CPUs and GPUs to cell phones, space or anything in-between. On the weekend I'm usually hanging out with friends and dogs. I'm a PC Gamer and enjoy playing a host of different games as well as watching my fair share of eSports.
